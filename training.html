Are you a born artist? Or did you just graduate as an artist or compositor?

TRAINING

  • Do not be frustrated by the lack of "experience" in doing what you like the most, acquire it with us! We may not be the oldest in the industry; but certainly, we'll model, shade, render and write the stories together, you and us.

  • The training is online, so you will not have the limitations of border closings, risk of infection or expensive legal migration paperwork. Also, true artists don't need to bother with visa, residency or nationality restrictions to do their jobs. So, let's leave all those obstacles to those who really seek to venture as immigrants due to economic factors, but not because they really want to develop their artistic talents.

    • REQUIREMENTS

  • A recent demo reel in either lighting, tracking and matchmoving, rotoscoping, or compositing.
  • Excellent understanding of your field of expertise.
    eg. for Lighting: Understanding of physically based rendering (PBR) techniques; real-time and pre-rendered lighting techniques (including dynamic lights, global illumination, ambient occlusion, and pre-baked light map solutions). A strong understanding of light, shadow, and colour, with the ability to use that understanding to create dynamic compositions. Manage rendering of all elements/AOV's of a shot; creating background/mattes, foreground/characters, lens effects and visual effects. Experience with production ready render engines (Renderman, Arnold, Vray). Prefered proficiency with Maya and/or Katana (or at least one major 3D software package like 3ds Max or C4D). Nuke and Photoshop.
    eg. for Compositing: Multi-pass compositing elements, passes or AOVs to integrate in live action footage; 3d compositing, 3d Camera Projection, will create mattes (cutouts) by keying out a green or blue screen.
    eg. for Tracking/Matchmove: Ability to track plates, familiarity using relevant tracking software such as 3DEqualizer, Nuke or a like.
    eg. for Roto-Paint: Ability to generate accurate articulate roto mattes, be able to track plates using 2D trackers or planar tracking methods, use frame by frame painting techniques, efficient use of matching motion blur, painting out rigs and wires for removal.
