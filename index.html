Helping Motion Picture and Film Directors to convert their ideas into top quality visual and audio contents for entertainment and advertising.

This is by mean of eye to detailed artistic:

  • Film and TV Vfx
  • Stereo Conversion
  • Lighting scenes in 3D with either V-ray, Arnold or Renderman to match Key, Fill and Rim lights to live action footage.
  • Selecting passes for rendering with V-ray or elements with Arnold; and using LPEs for outputting light into specific AOVs with Renderman.
  • Multi-pass compositing V-ray elements, Arnold passes or Renderman AOVs to integrate in live action footage;
  • 3d compositing, 3d Camera Projection, Point and Camera Tracking, Lens distortion correction, Matchmoving, shot Stabilization and remote farm rendering in the clouds with Zync by Google;
  • Key animation, Chromakeying, Color Correction and Grading, Rotoscoping and Painting.

ABOUT US

Clients' safety is first!

We were born as an initiative in response to the need on the part of companies in the Motion Picture and Films industry and Advertisers to contract post-production and editing services remotely. This is for the purpose of avoiding Covid-19 infections that could result from direct face-to-face contact and thus protect the health of our clients and representatives of Daylight Studio.

A united remote artistic cultural diversity

We are a group of 3D Artists and Compositors working remotely, but all united through our representative entity of Daylight Studio. The cultural diversity of our collaborators contributes unique ideas for the artistic conceptualization of the visual effects that our clients demand.

Best artistic service for your budget

We practice transparency in every business relationship, bringing the best of our artistic knowledge to our clients and adjusted to their investment budgets.

A happy client means more business

We try to satisfy our clients so that the business relationship is repetitive and brings consecutive benefits to both parties. We know that a happy client means more business for us, that's why we strive to provide them with a service that guarantees 100% their post-production objectives.

Our clients deliver their best content to their audience

By providing a quality service, it leads to our business success; that is why our goal at Daylight Studio is to make sure that our clients deliver their best audio visual content to their audience.

We train new artists to gain experience and recognition

In addition to offering post-production services, at Daylight we are also committed to training new artists who want to gain experience and professional recognition. Currently, very few studios offer this type of opportunity to new graduate Compositors and 3D Artists starting as a junior.

From Latam & Associates to Daylight Studio

In 2001, Joshua Selva founded Latam & Associates dedicated to the business of print publishing of airport and seaport business directories. It was not until 2020 that Joshua Selva, in partnership with Martha Selva, decided to found Daylight Studio to offer post-production services and elaboration of visual effects for films, video and commercials. Being Daylight Studio a central channel to manage contracts for the development of post-production services to be executed by its wide network of affiliated artists, all working remotely and thus prevent transmission by Covid-19 for the safety of clients and Daylight Studio staff.
